Who We Are

BrikX was founded with a bold idea — that a single company could master both the physical world of construction and the digital world of technology. Since 2020, we've delivered over 130 projects across 5 countries, with one consistent standard: excellence.

Our team brings together civil engineers, architects, software developers, and UX designers — all working under one roof to deliver end-to-end solutions that others can't.
